Built to stay current

A daily-refresh workflow—not a one-time spreadsheet.

The included Python pipeline searches Government of Canada Job Bank feeds and federal student programs, USAJOBS, official Greenhouse/Lever/Ashby/SmartRecruiters/Workday/SuccessFactors endpoints, Canadian law-firm recruiting pages, public internship feeds, and optional authorized search APIs. It then filters, deduplicates, ranks, writes cold emails, and publishes only when strict source-health checks pass.

Deployment is required: the daily schedule begins after this project is placed in a GitHub repository and the workflow is enabled.

Coverage & methodology

What this board does—and does not claim

Résumé matching

Scores emphasize your combined commerce-law program, RBC client and compliance work, 9.5/10 CGPA, trilingual communication, and analytics toolkit.

Eligibility screening

Class year, U.S. authorization, citizenship, ABA-school, Canadian work eligibility, co-op registration, and term-length issues are surfaced rather than hidden.

Daily discovery

The deployed workflow checks Government of Canada Job Bank searches and student programs, provincial and parliamentary programs, U.S. federal sources, official employer ATS endpoints, Canadian law-firm recruiting pages, public feeds, and credentialed search APIs. Each source is logged as success, empty, skipped, or error.

Fail-visible publication

Curated records are not counted as live discovery. A refresh is blocked when Job Bank produces no relevant result, too few source families complete, or too few unique live opportunities survive screening.

Important limitation

No system can guarantee every job posted across the internet. The project avoids prohibited scraping, labels aggregator records as discovery leads, and provides auditable source-health files rather than claiming universal coverage.
